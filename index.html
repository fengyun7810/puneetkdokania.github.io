About Me:
I have been working as a postdoctoral research fellow in Prof. Torr's group (Torr Vision Group), University of Oxford, since 01st August 2016.

In the past I was a PhD student at CentraleSupelec and INRIA Saclay from October 2012 to March 2016. During my PhD, I worked under the supervision of Prof. M. Pawan Kumar and co-supervision of Prof. Nikos Paragios in the intersection of machine learning, inference, and computer vision.
Research Interests:
Machine Learning, Inference, Approximation algorithms, and Computer Vision.
