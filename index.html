About Me:
I am a PhD student at Ecole Centrale de Paris and INRIA Saclay since October 2012. I work under the supervision of M. Pawan Kumar and co-supervision of Nikos Paragios in the intersection of machine learning, inference, and computer vision at CVC. I am funded by the doctoral scholarship of Ecole Centrale Paris.

Currently, I am working as a visiting researcher in the SIERRA team headed by Francis Bach. I am working with Simon Lacoste-Julien on the problems related to the optimization of SSVM.

Research Interests:
Machine Learning, Inference, Approximation algorithms, and Computer Vision.
