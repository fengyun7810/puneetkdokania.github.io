About Me:
I am a PhD student at Ecole Centrale de Paris and INRIA Saclay since October 2012. I am a member of the CVC lab at Ecole Centrale, Galen Team at INRIA, and Optimization for Vision and Learning group (OVAL) at Oxford University.

I work under the supervision of M. Pawan Kumar and co-supervision of Nikos Paragios in the intersection of machine learning, inference, and computer vision. I am funded by the doctoral scholarship of Ecole Centrale Paris.
Research Interests:
Machine Learning, Inference, Approximation algorithms, and Computer Vision.
